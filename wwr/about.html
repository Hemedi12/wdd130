ABOUT US

Since owner / manager Jon Dahl's first trip here with the legendary Kingsley Holgate on the Afrika Odyssey trip in 1993 , the Uganda tourism industry has developed at a very steady rate and Nile River Explorers has led the way in adventure tourism. Staffed with a team of highly experienced travel and activity professionals with strong affiliations across the industry, Nile River Explorers offers the largest range of river and riverbank activities to individual travelers, groups and businesses. The White Nile offers possibly the best grade 5 white water rafting in the world, and we offer experiences on the Nile that allow every level of thrill seeker and every age group a chance to enjoy this beautiful river. We are partnered with the finest kayak school on the continent, Kayak the Nile are strongly associated with the best of other local adventure operators.
